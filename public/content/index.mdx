        <LeadParagraph>Through digital transformation, NHS BSA is working to improve the process of creating and reviewing the Medical Certificate of Cause of Death (MCCD).</LeadParagraph>
    </GridColumn>
    <GridColumn width={'1/2'}>
        Currently in alpha but due for launch in April 2024, the new digitised MCCD service will:
        
        * be designed around users
        * help streamline the workflow of attending practitioners and medical examiners
        * enable reporting and improved insights
        * vastly improve efficiency
        * better support the bereaved
    </GridColumn>
